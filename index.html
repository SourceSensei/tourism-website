Nina Tolkunova

Portugal, a welcoming and charming country, has become a popular destination for many individuals, including myself. Situated on the edge of the earth, Portugal elicits a range of emotions that are difficult to ignore. As a guide in Portugal since 2008, I have acquired high organizational skills, particularly when working with large tourist groups. Moreover, I possess a unique approach to children and have extensive experience in the field of individual tourism, where I can tailor my services to satisfy even the most discerning client. Additionally, I specialize in planning individual tours throughout Portugal and can cater to any specific needs or preferences. I also offer translation services for summit meetings, further showcasing my proficiency in the language. My area of expertise includes a variety of services, such as organizing group and individual excursions, meeting clients at the airport, arranging transfers, accompanying clients to business meetings, exhibitions, and conferences, car rental services, conference organization, interpretation, and translation. I am registered on Experty.Tourister.Ru and have been providing my services since November 7, 2013.
