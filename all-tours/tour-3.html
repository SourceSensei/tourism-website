EVORA. For women's happiness

There is no exact date of the founding of the city, but the fact that it arose in antiquity is indisputable, as evidenced by the remains of the Roman temple of Diana in the upper part of the city, on a charming square with a magnificent panorama. Although the numerous monuments of Évora were built in complete disregard for the layout of the city, nevertheless they organically fit into its structure, and therefore a walk through its streets is a real pleasure, allowing you to return from the past to the present. The original core of the city is limited by the fortress walls, outside of them are modern streets, the pattern of which resembles the layout of the ancient city. We will visit: in the CE Cathedral, chapel shower ossush, Church of St. Francisco. Prasa do Giraldo, etc. and at the end of the tour I will definitely tell you why people come to this city for "women's happiness"!
