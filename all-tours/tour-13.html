Corporate party in ... TRAIN N 28

Tram 28 is another symbol of Lisbon, which even the ubiquitous progress has not overcome! The poet of the Russian diaspora, Yuri Ivask / 1910 - 1986 /, probably dejected by the communist coup in October 1917 in Russia, wrote the poem "Lisbon Tram" after a trip to Portugal: Filled to overflowing with the poor The fifth lost tram. The old accountants are angry And the factory farm: - Hey, get up. Make room for the lady, little one, And in their language - rapash! Rejoice, proletarian - Portuguese, But you have to, and you will give in the face! Today, the tram is one of the biggest tourist attractions in Lisbon, where the proletariat is virtually non-existent. Preserved as one of the city's most beautiful relics, this somewhat antiquated mode of transport carries fewer Portuguese and more tourists over Lisbon's mythical seven hills and scenic neighborhoods. I offer travel by tram number 28 ONLY FOR YOU! The holiday in the tram will be accompanied by music, you will be able to taste Portuguese snacks and drinks! We will make stops near the most beautiful viewing platforms in Lisbon, and I will be able to tell a lot of interesting things about the city. OH, LET'S RIDE!
