Living Water

THERMAL WATERS - THE PRIVILEGE OF KINGS! Portugal is famous for its large number of thermal resorts, many of which have been known since Roman times! In recent years, Portuguese thermal resorts have become a popular tourist destination, where even quite healthy people come to just relax. Thermal waters in the resorts of Portugal can be called real living water! Unlike traditional medicine, thermal water treatments are procedures that can have a healing effect on the body and at the same time give pleasure. In addition, there are usually a number of special "leisure" procedures in thermal spas. At different thermal spas, depending on the specific procedure, prices fluctuate, on average ranging from 5 to 15 euros per session, however, of course, some procedures are much more expensive. The thermal waters of Caldas de Rainha are located in a picturesque place 80 km from Lisbon. They say that even five minutes of looking at a beautiful natural landscape can be enough to overcome stress and restore peace of mind. The city of Caldas da Rainha was founded by the Queen of Portugal, Leonora of Avisa. I suggest you spend one day in one of the provincial cities of little Portugal, not only as an educational walk, but also with health benefits. At your request, we can look into the nearby towns of Obidos or Nazare. If you need a longer stay, I can always help you organize this type of holiday, and not only in Caldas da Rainha.
