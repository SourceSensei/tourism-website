Palace of Queluz / Versailles in Portugal

Have you been to Versailles yet? It's okay, being in the "backyards of Europe", i.e. in Portugal, you can catch up! The construction of the palace began in 1747 and was completed only in 1786. The palace impresses with the perfection of its layout. Numerous halls of the palace, filled with works of art, are decorated with mirrors, which reflect the luxurious baroque decoration. the gardens surrounding the palace stretch over many hectares with their bosquets, fountains, ponds and a boat channel lined with luxurious tiled panels with pastoral scenes. Once upon a time, a brilliant life of the royal court flowed in these places, trying, albeit unsuccessfully, to surpass the courts of Europe in the 18th century in splendor. Nostalgia for those irrevocable times is still heard in the rustle of leaves from the breeze coming from the sea.
