Bow to Fatima

FATIMA. A place of pilgrimage comparable to Santiago de Campostela in Spain or Lourdes in France. On May 12 and October 12, huge crowds of pilgrims come here to commemorate the appearance of Our Lady to the Three Shepherds. I invite you to participate in Miss Candles. This must be seen. There are things that cannot be described in words. If you come to Fatima on any other day, you are unlikely to get the feeling that in front of you is a shrine, "the altar of humanity", as John Paul II called this place. But, having arrived on the evening of May 12 or October 12, you will have no doubts that you are where the voice of your heart called you. I would call this place the purgatory of the human soul. Someone may remark: “But is this a Catholic shrine? “I won’t argue, once upon a time the Christian brothers were divided into Catholics and Orthodox, people then remembered the wonderful saying from the Bible: “GOD IS LOVE!” ? Most tourists, however, are most amazed by the faith of sinners approaching the temple on their knees. As an offering for the miracles performed by the Mother of God, candles are lit. There are thousands and thousands of them. Good luck, bow to Fatima! PS The rules for booking excursions on the site oblige to indicate the cost.
