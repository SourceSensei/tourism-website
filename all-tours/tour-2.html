I am interested in visiting Lisbon and would like to engage in a sightseeing experience that involves exploring the city by car, with designated stops for pedestrian exploration.

This tour is a car-walking tour. We start the sightseeing day at your hotel, after which we set off on a journey! Let's start our acquaintance with Lisbon from the main avenue of the country, Avenida Liberdade. This is the main artery of the city, but the traffic does not distract you from the beauty of this former pedestrian alley. The paving of the square is done in a typical Portuguese manner - black square slabs on a white background. Liberty Avenue is an indispensable place to visit for all those arriving in Lisbon. Liberty Avenue passes into the Marquis of Pombal Square, in the center of which rises a majestic monument to the Marquis of Pombal. The marquis proudly looks at the modern life of the townspeople. Behind the monument is the greenery of the Edward VII park. From here you can see the entire underlying territory of the city and even the silver-blue surface of the Tagus River. Next, Rossio will appear before us, the central square surrounded by fountains and always filled with people hurrying about their business and idly walking, flower sellers, flocks of pigeons, long accustomed to the hustle and bustle of the city. Chiado, viewing platforms, the Church of St. Roch, the Santa Justa elevator, Baixa, all of this certainly deserves your attention! Our tour will continue at the walls of the Cathedral of Lisbon. The territory where the church was originally built was inhabited by peoples of different faiths: Romans, Visigoths and Arabs. The castle of St. George is located relatively close to the fortress, conquered by King Afonso Henriques from the Moors. Now there is a large park where peacocks walk. This territory unites the quarters of Alfama, Grasse and Moraria, where the Portuguese nation was born. Along the embankment of the Tagus River, we will quickly reach the Belen area. There we will visit the monastery of Hieronymites, Belenskaya Tower, a monument to the Discoverers. If you have a sweet tooth, then you will have a great opportunity to try the most delicious cakes in Portugal! Changes in the program are always possible according to your desire.
