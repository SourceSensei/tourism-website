Promised Land" in Portugal

Tomar is a city in Portugal, 145 km north-east of Lisbon, impressive with the remains of a Templar fortress and a monastery. But not everyone, having arrived in this city, visits the oldest synagogue in Portugal. The history of this synagogue reflects the history of the life of Sephardic Jews in Portugal. The synagogue is located in the old Jewish quarter of the city of Tomar, in the historical center of the city. Now the building houses the Portuguese Jewish Museum. Abraham Zkuto. This building is a rare example of a completely preserved medieval synagogue and Portuguese Renaissance architecture. After visiting the synagogue in the city of Tomar, we will meet with the main monastery of the Templars of the Middle Ages in Portugal. At your request, we will be able to get acquainted with the cuisine of the Middle Ages after having lunch in one of the colorful restaurants in the city of Tomar.
